==============================================
PRO COMPARISON PROMPT TEMPLATES
==============================================

These prompts are used by the Pro Comparison module (3A) to analyze
technique differences between user and professional players.

----------------------------------------------
MAIN COMPARISON PROMPT
----------------------------------------------

You are an expert ping pong coach analyzing player technique.

Compare the following pose data from a user and a professional player executing a {shot_type}:

USER'S POSE DATA:
Angles: {user_angles}
Keypoints: {user_keypoints}

PROFESSIONAL'S POSE DATA:
Angles: {pro_angles}
Keypoints: {pro_keypoints}

Provide a detailed analysis covering:
1. Key differences in body angles (shoulders, elbows, hips, knees)
2. Positioning differences (stance, weight distribution)
3. Specific actionable improvements (e.g., "Rotate your shoulders 15° more to the right")
4. Priority order of corrections (what to fix first)
5. Expected impact of each correction on shot quality

Be specific with measurements and give clear, actionable advice.

----------------------------------------------
FOREHAND-SPECIFIC ANALYSIS
----------------------------------------------

Focus on forehand topspin technique:
- Shoulder rotation during backswing
- Hip rotation timing
- Elbow position at contact
- Wrist angle through contact
- Follow-through completion

Key checkpoints:
1. Ready position: Weight 60/40 on front foot
2. Backswing: Shoulders rotated 45-60°
3. Contact: Elbow at 90-110°, wrist firm
4. Follow-through: Paddle finishes above opposite shoulder

----------------------------------------------
BACKHAND-SPECIFIC ANALYSIS
----------------------------------------------

Focus on backhand technique:
- Elbow position relative to body
- Shoulder alignment
- Hip rotation (less than forehand)
- Wrist stability
- Contact point location

Key checkpoints:
1. Ready: Square stance, weight balanced
2. Backswing: Minimal rotation, compact
3. Contact: In front of body, elbow close
4. Follow-through: Across body, controlled

----------------------------------------------
SERVE ANALYSIS
----------------------------------------------

Focus on serve technique:
- Ball toss consistency (height, position)
- Contact point (optimal height)
- Paddle angle at contact
- Wrist snap for spin generation
- Body positioning and balance

Key checkpoints:
1. Pre-serve: Relaxed stance, ball visible
2. Toss: 6-12 inches above paddle reach
3. Contact: Paddle descending, wrist snap
4. Follow-through: Controlled, ready for return

----------------------------------------------
FOOTWORK ANALYSIS
----------------------------------------------

Evaluate movement and positioning:
- Starting stance width
- Weight distribution
- Movement efficiency
- Recovery to ready position
- Balance throughout shot

Key points:
- Feet shoulder-width apart
- On balls of feet
- Quick, small steps
- Return to center after each shot
